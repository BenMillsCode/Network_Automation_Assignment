#Configuring SNMP Example

snmp-server community ANT ro SNMP_ACL
snmp-server location ANT_manager
snmp-server contact ANT_Admin
snmp-server host 209.165.200.238 version 2c ANT
snmp-server enable traps ospf errors
snmp-server enable traps ospf state-change

ip access-list standard SNMP_ACL
  permit 209.165.200.238
